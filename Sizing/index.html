Method: ISA-75.01 / IEC 60534-2 for compressible (steam) flow.   ΔPcrit = FL² × P1 × 0.5.   Cv = W / (63.3 × FP × √(ΔPeff × ρ₁)).   Kv = Cv × 0.865.   Water: Kv = Q / √ΔP, Cv = Kv / 0.865.   Body sized to next standard NB where rated Cv ≥ design Cv.
